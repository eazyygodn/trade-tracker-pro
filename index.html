How it works
Live in 5 minutes

No servers to manage, no broker API keys needed. Just download, install, and trade.

1
Create your account
Sign up on the dashboard. Copy your User ID and generate an API key — takes 30 seconds.
2
Download the installer
Download our one-click Windows installer. Extract and double-click install.bat — it finds your MT4/MT5 automatically.
3
Attach EA to chart
Restart MT5, drag TradeTrackerPro onto any chart, enable Algo Trading, and you're done.
4
Trade normally
Open a trade — it appears on your dashboard within seconds. Every open, close, and SL/TP change is captured automatically.
